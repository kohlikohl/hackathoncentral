Our Idea

  • To help out-of-towners and Londoners to find the most suitable place to live.
    • Students
    • Families
    • Young Professionals
    • Pensioners
  • Mashup of multiple API's
  • Generating index, displaying on a map
  • Drill down to postcode level
  • Social feedback
  • Usable, accessible on mobile devices

Our API's

  • Policing data, Transport data from different API's?
  • Local Government Association’s esd-toolkit
    • ~15+ endpoints
      • Pollution
      • Crime
      • Public services
      • Education
      • Cost of living
      • Employment
    • London Borough level

Our Tech

  • Front-end
    • Google Closure Library
    • Google Maps API
    • Wikimapia.org API
  • Back-end
    • Node.js
    • Express.js
    • hogan-express, (Mustache templating)
  • Hosting
    • Heroku
